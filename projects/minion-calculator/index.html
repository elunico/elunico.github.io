Note 1: this assumes you have enough fuel for however long you want to know the coins for. In other words, if you say fuel=Foul Flesh the Earnings Per Week calculation will assume you have a weeks worth or (24/5) * 7 = 34 foul flesh to put into the minion

Note 2: Number of drops per action might not be what you think. A minion takes 1 action to place/spawn an item/mob and then 1 action to mine/kill the item or mob. Therefore, whatever drops it gets (if its 4 snowballs, 1 diamond, 1 chicken + 1 feather + 1 egg etc.) it only gets every other action. This means the items per action should be (number of drops / 2). However, if you are using a farm setup where something else kills the mob this results in every action being used to spawn and every mob being killed, so if you are harvesting drops this way you can just use the number of drops without dividing by 2

Note 3: Currently the app does not pay attention to (super)compactors but in the future it will be able to tell you if the minion will fill up in a day or week even with the compactors

Note 4: If you click "edit minion" the minion will be removed and the form will be changed to the values of that minion. This streamlines the process of changing minions but it will overwrite anything in the "Add Minion" section and will change the order of the minions. The edit button is a simple convenience and is equivalent to deleting and readding a minion with the added benefit that the form is automatically filled out with the stats of the minion that gets removed, so you can change them easily.
